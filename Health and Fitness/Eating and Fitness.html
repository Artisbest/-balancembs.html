Good health and wellness is not a pie in the sky idea. It is a holistic balance of a person's mind body and Soul.

To accomplish this the physical health and personal wellness must be examinined.


Nutritional needs

Physical health looks at Nutritional needs and Personal hygiene
Nutritional needs begins with ensuring your that diet is balance
with calcuim, iron and vitamins
Also Fruits, green vegetables, proteins, carbohydrates etc.

Personal hygiene

Personal hygiene speaks to the odour eminating from your body (arm pits, mouth, vagina or Testicle).
This odour alerts you that something is wrong with your body and you need to try and seek medical help.

Personal wellness

Personal wellness examines the well being of a person;
How is a person maintaing their body?
Is the person maintaing a healthy lifestyle?
What is the person state of mind?
Is the person making time to exercise?
Is the person emotionally stable/unstable?
Is the person making personal time for themselves?
Is the person making time for Rest and Relaxation?
Does the person has spiritual values to aid him/her in their darkest moments or wilderness experience?
