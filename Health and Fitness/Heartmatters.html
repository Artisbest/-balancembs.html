HEART MATTERS


In our society today many men and women like the idea of being in a relationship for many
reasons but fail to understand the dangerous waters they are dilving into when they take
for granted matters of the heart.


DO'S AND DONT'S AND RELATIONSHIPS

Do's in relationship:

  1. Be Faithful to your partner

  2. Be committed to your partner

  3. Be considerate to your partner

  4. Be understanding to each other

  5. Love each other

  6. Respect each other


Dont's in relationship:
  • Taking each other for granted

  • Dishonesty

  • Cheating

  • Possessive partner

  • Partner with no vision
